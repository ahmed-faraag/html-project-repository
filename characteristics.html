Big data can be described with the following characteristics: the sound The amount of data created and stored. The size of the data determines the value and potential visibility, and whether or not it can be considered large data. variety The type and nature of the data. This helps people who analyze it to use the resulting insight effectively. Big data is derived from text, images, audio and video; In addition, it complements the missing pieces by merging the data. the speed The speed with which data is created and processed to meet the demands and challenges that lie in the path of growth and development. Big data is often available in real time. Compared to small data, big data is continuously produced. There are two types of speed related to big data which are generation frequency, processing frequency, recording and publishing. Reliability It is the expanded definition of big data, which indicates data quality and data value. The quality of the captured data data can vary greatly, which affects accurate analysis.
